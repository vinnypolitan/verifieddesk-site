What goes wrong in this market, and what does it cost?

$499

24 to 48 hours

The downside case, built from evidence rather than intuition. Regulatory exposure, competitive dynamics that can flip, demand risks, and the cost of the most common failure modes.

  • Regulatory risk: pending rules, enforcement trends, and jurisdictions with active scrutiny
