The research protocol

Why our numbers survive scrutiny

  1. Register

    Every algorithm version is committed and tagged before its backtest fires, byte-exact. Results always trace to the exact code that produced them.

  2. Backtest

    Runs execute on QuantConnect and LEAN against 16 years of data at the resolution the strategy needs, down to tick, and land in an append-only catalog. A test is never quietly re-run until it says something nicer.

  3. Validate

    Out-of-sample windows, blind multi-year evaluations, frozen-parameter pre-period tests, and cross-market checks separate signal from curve fitting.

  4. Stress

    Slippage stress at multiple cost levels, parameter stability sweeps, and reproducibility reruns. Fragile results get labeled fragile.

  5. Report, or retract

    Validated results ship with their full context. Results that fail later interrogation are retracted on record, not buried. You get the same honesty as a client.
