The standard

Three rules every report follows

  1. No uncited claims

    Every factual statement links to a named source: a filing, a dataset, a published study, a company page. You can follow any footnote and check it.

  2. Confidence is labeled

    Strong evidence, single source, and estimate are marked differently. You always know how much weight a claim can carry.

  3. Methodology included

    A short note explains what was searched, what was excluded, and where the walls were. Dead ends are disclosed, not papered over.
